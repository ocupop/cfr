We are excited to present the new and improved CFR Signature Grip lineup. The original black Signature Grip was so popular that we've opened up a full range of colors and flavors to finish off your cockpit in style. With raised logo graphics for superior grip that resists snow and ice buildup, the Signature Grip is also slightly narrower than our original CFR Grip for an even more direct connection to your machine while still installing easily over grip heaters.

SPECS:

  • Functional graphics for maximum grip performance

  • Compatible with hand warmers.

  • Fits over straight ends or handlebars that have pre bent hooks.

  • 5 7/8"/ 149mm - Length

  • 13/16" / 20mm - Inside diameter

  • Sold per pair

The Signature grip fits all handlebars including ski doo's smaller diameter handlebar.
