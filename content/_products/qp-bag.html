The CFR QP Bag offers 25 liters (1526 cu-ins) of waterproof carrying capacity and fits snugly in all of the CFR tunnel racks. The QP is a telescoping bag that doubles in size with a quick zipper extension to carry up to 50 liters (3052 cu-ins) of gear.

 * The new #10 YKK waterproof top zipper is long-lasting, can be opened and closed in the most demanding situations, and keeps your gear dry.

Product Specs

  • Cordura waterproof fabric.
  • Waterproof YKK zippers.
  • 25 liter (1526 cu-ins) capacity. Un-zip the extension and up to 50 liters (3052 cu-ins) of capacity is possible.
  • Clips right into all CFR rack systems and is easily removable.
  • Shovel holder on the top enables a shovel to be carried on the deep days for quick access.
  • Convenient carrying handle.
  • Individual inside pocket.
