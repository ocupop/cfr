This kit allows you to use your stock post and removes the height adjustable post on all Arctic cat snowmobile with an adjustable height steering post. Simply cut while still on the sled and install the new T style billet block. You will also need to get a riser height of your choice. Eliminates 90% of the slop found in the stock adjustable post and saves over 1 Lb depending of the type of risers you choose.
