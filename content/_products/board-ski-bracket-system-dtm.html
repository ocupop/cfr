We have been working on the direct tunnel mount bracket for the past 5 years. The countless molds and R and D have made this our biggest project to date. We are happy to finally get you the new Direct tunnel mount Boardski bracket System.

The demands and expectations of our users of a snowboard/ski bracket are huge. That’s why having the CFR DTM Boardski system released, with our quality and customers expectations all in the design, is such great news! The ability to carry either a pair of skis and or a snowboards with no other rack required is now a reality. Just buy this bracket, bolt it to your snowmobile, throw your skis and or snowboard on and go. This is for the minimalist who doesn't want the rack on the back and just wants to carry his skis and or snowboards depending on their fancy for the day.

Product Specs

  • The CFR DTM Boardski bracket bolts to the side of most snowmobiles. (Does not work on 07 and newer Polaris snowmobiles) or ski doo revs.
  • Two Nylon infused polyurethane brackets carry snowboard and or skis securely and safely.
  • Custom made injection molded nylon straps keep your shit tight.
  • CFR uses custom Ratchets designed to be used under very high tensile loads with thicker springs and ratcheting mechanisms.
  • Carries either a pair of skis and poles or a snowboard.
  • Another DTM Boardski bracket system can be purchased to carry up to two skis and or snowboards.
  • Top vertical Holes line up with pre drilled Ski Doo XM’s hole pattern.
