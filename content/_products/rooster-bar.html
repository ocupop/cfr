The 2020 Rooster 2.0 bar is the next generation of the original Rooster bar. The oversized 1 1/8” clamp along with the 7075 aluminum create a strong yet damp feeling handlebar. The 28” width is designed for riders with narrower shoulders or riders who wanted a narrower handlebar that was not available. The Flat space still allows enough room for all of your controls and brake lever.

Important Notes:

*The Rooster bar is an oversize 1 1/8" in the center. A 7/8" to 1 1/8" adapter might be needed depending on the snowmobile riser you have*

Product Specs

  • Clamp- 1 1/8”
  • Width- 28”
  • Rise- 0 Degree’s
  • Bend-0 Degree’s
  • PullBack-12 Degree
