Ad a little more Metal to the rear tunnel of your snowmobile. The CFR XP/XM Blades are designed to be fastened to the outside or inside rear tunnel of your Ski Doo snowmobile.

 Blades ad 50% more strength to the rear tunnel. The XP/XM Blades are recommended if you travel on bumpy roads carrying lots of extra weight on the rear tunnel of your snowmobile.

 Blades also available for Arctic Cat M series 2005-2008 and Yamaha Nytros as well.

Product Specs

  • Anatomically designed to fit the rear tunnel
  • Blades all come in anodized black.
  • Come complete with 1 right/1 left side and with stainless steel rivets.
  • Made in Canada.
