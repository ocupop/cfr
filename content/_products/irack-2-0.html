The iRack 2.0 is a hybrid of the new-generation Mountain Rack and our original iRack. The bigger carrying capacity allows you to carry extra gear and positions the CFR rack mounted bracket kits further apart to better secure skis and snowboards. The low profile design keeps your toys lower to the snowmobile for improved handling when you're loaded up and the strong, light weight design is almost bomb proof. The iRack 2.0 works with all CFR rack mounted bracket kits and CFR bags.
